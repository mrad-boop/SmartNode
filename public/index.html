How SmartNode Works

A step-by-step explanation of the FIFO matrix, how payments flow, and what you can earn when your network grows.

① Getting Started

1

Connect Your Solana Wallet

Use Phantom or Solflare browser extension, or the Phantom mobile app. Your wallet address is your unique identity on the network — no email, no KYC. On mobile, use the in-app browser to connect directly.

2

Choose a Username & Register

Pick a unique username (3–20 characters, letters/numbers/underscore). Registration is free. If you arrived via someone's referral link, your upline matrix is automatically set from their network — otherwise the system default is used.

3

Pay the System Fee & 6 Level Activations

To become fully active you complete 7 payments in USDT on Solana:
— 1× System Fee (sent to the platform)
— 6× Level payments (sent directly to your 6 upline nodes, in order L1 → L6)
Each payment goes wallet-to-wallet. SmartNode never holds your funds.

4

Share Your Referral Link & Earn

Once all 6 levels are paid your referral link activates. Anyone who registers through your link will have you at position L1 of their upline — meaning every level they pay sends USDT directly to your wallet first.

② The FIFO Matrix Explained

FIFO = First In, First Out. Every new user's upline is derived from their referrer's upline matrix. The queue rotates automatically: your referrer moves to L6 in your chain, and their 5 existing uplines shift from L2→L1 to cover L1→L5. This means the oldest active participants always appear first in the payment chain — the system is provably fair and never requires admin intervention.

How the Matrix Builds

Example: Alice has upline [U1, U2, U3, U4, U5, Alice]

When Bob registers via Alice's link, Bob's upline becomes:
[U2, U3, U4, U5, Alice, Bob's_referrer=Alice]
— Alice moves to L5, and the chain shifts one position forward. Bob pays L1 (U2) first, all the way to L6 (Alice).

Net Profit (after 70 USDT activation) +10,850 USDT
Important notes:
— These figures are illustrative only and assume ideal conditions (every user refers exactly 3 people).
— Actual earnings depend on your network's activity and the FIFO queue position at each level.
— All payments are peer-to-peer; SmartNode is a coordination layer, not a custodian.
— The amount per level is set by the platform administrator and may differ from the example above.
